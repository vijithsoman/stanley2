Welcome

Welcome to Just a Minute Jesus — a place where hearts are stirred, lives are changed, and Jesus is lifted high. Whether you're new to faith, searching for truth, or already walking with the Lord, you're invited to encounter Jesus in a powerful and personal way.

Our Mission

  • 🕊️ To witness for Christ.
  • 🕯️ To shine His light in a dark world.
  • ❤️ To remind every soul that Jesus saves, heals, and is coming again.

No matter where you are on your journey, a single minute with Jesus can change everything.

About Us

A Voice in the Wilderness. A Light to the World.

Just a Minute Jesus is a Christ-centered platform sharing love, truth, and power through short, impactful video testimonies. Even one minute in His presence brings transformation, healing, hope, and salvation.

What We Believe

  • Jesus Christ is the Son of God — fully God and fully man.
  • He lived a sinless life, died for our sins, and rose again.
  • Salvation comes by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God.
  • The Holy Spirit empowers us to live holy lives.
  • Jesus will return in glory and power.
